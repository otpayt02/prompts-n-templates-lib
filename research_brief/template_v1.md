# Research Brief Prompt Template — v1

## Purpose

Turn a question or topic into a focused research brief with scope, source expectations, assumptions, and decision-useful output.

## Required context fields

- `research_question`: What needs to be answered.
- `decision_context`: Why this research matters.
- `source_requirements`: What kinds of sources are acceptable.
- `recency_need`: Stable, recent, or current.

## Preferred output structure

1. Research question.
2. Scope boundaries.
3. Key subquestions.
4. Source plan.
5. Findings structure.
6. Risks and uncertainty.
7. Final answer format.

## Critique dimensions

- Is the research question narrow enough?
- Are source expectations clear?
- Is the output useful for a decision?
- Are unknowns and uncertainty explicit?
